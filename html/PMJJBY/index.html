Pradhan Mantri Jeevan Jyoti Beema Yojana(PMJJBY)
PMJJBY

Introduction

  • PMJJBY is a one-year life insurance scheme, offering coverage for death. You can sell this policy to Airtel Payments bank savings account customer using Mitra App. You just need the customer’s account linked mobile number.

Login to the Mitra App and Bank Section, Tap on Pradhan Mantri Jeevan Jyoti Beema Yojana (PMJJBY) .

Enter Airtel Payments Bank Customer’s Account linked Mobile No , Ask Customer Check the declaration and Tap on Go .

Ask customer to provide Verification code, enter it here and Tap on Verify .

Customer details appear from airtel payments bank system, enter nominee details and enter address if address is different.

If nominee is minor please enter guardian details here, Tap on Proceed.

Ask customer to read the declaration , terms and condition and also tell him about auto debit agreement for next year renewal of policy, as customer to tick all and take biometric authentication to complete the process .

Enter retailers' mPIN and Tap on Done .

Transaction has been completed successfully.

And policy will be issued and Policy link will be sent to customer mobile number .

Points to Remember:

  • PMJJBY is a pure term insurance policy, which covers only mortality.
  • Age limit for this policy is 18 to 50 years.
  • Policy is priced at 330 rupees. (Remember never charge the customer more than the actual amount as violators will be punished).
  • Risk cover under this policy is of rupees 2 lakhs if the policyholder passes away due to any reason.
  • Customers cannot avail the benefit of this policy within 45 days of issuance.
  • Policy expiry date is May 31, 2019, whether the policy is purchased in any month of the year. For example, if the policy is bought in July 2018, the policy will end on May 31, 2019, meaning the policy term will remain for only 10 months. During the purchase of insurance, the consent of auto debit will also be taken for next year.
  • This is only available for Airtel Payments Bank savings account customers.
  • Only one policy is issued per customer.
  • For more information dial 12144 or contact your sales team.
  • The same insurance will be valid only for a customer, even if it has been enrolled by any bank's savings account.
